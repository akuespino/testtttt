Background of the Project
Our proposed project aims to provide various solutions to the current problem our partner institution is facing, which is the surge of HIV cases and the aftermath brought by COVID, especially in the youth. This project helps in informing and in enhancing victims, target audience about this topic by helding an awareness talk about the increasing cases of HIV/AIDS to the community. We, in partnership with the MAAAI, focus on the positive impact of this project proposal which is the enhancement of sexual education amongst the youth and how this will help in the prevention of HIV/AIDS cases.

Significance
Solving this problem is vastly necessary for the welfare and lives of the patients that the institution has taken under its wing and for our community. This proposed project will help improve the livelihood of the patients affected and help prevent the further continuation of the viruses. Having the right knowledge will help them be aware of this problem and avoid being in cases like these. This problem has existed ever since the institution started growing and improving. This can help the audience enhance their kindness, love and knowledge about the problems of our society. This will boost their confidence to help those in need and with the resources we have due to technological advancements, the project proposal will be a success in its efficient and effective manner of spreading the message we intend to deliver.

Problem Identification
HIV/AIDS has been one of the most, if not the most, pressing concerns and problems in our partner institution. In this context, the youth’s lives have been greatly affected by this presented illness. This problem has been identified as a sexually-transmitted disease and its rise in cases has been of great concern. Our best answer to the problem is help in the prevention of the said illness in hopes of HIV/AIDS cases to lessen.

Proposed Intervention of the Project Proposal
In order to solve the problem of the increasing HIV/Aids cases in the youth is to provide better understanding about the issues to others. The proposed solution will be effective as it can help the people in the community to be well aware of the risks and prevention so as to not worsen the situation. It is better than other alternatives because it will directly inform others on how to deal with such problems and what we can do to avoid experiencing the complications. It would take courage and perseverance in order for the project to work as it will take some time and patience for the project to fully work and be effective.
