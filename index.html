-Mission-

The reason why the organization exists is to help fight the global issue on the spreading of HIV/Aids.
Its advocacy supports the well-being of the people to prevent such illnesses and maintain good health.
The services being provided are giving out free medications, HIV tests, training, and peer education to guide the patients.
Its main goal is to reduce the number of people contracting HIV/Aids as to make their life secure and safe from sickness.







History
Dr. Jordana P. Ramiterre helped in organizing, funding, and supporting this program, while the Global Funds has been known for the institution’s financial trust fund since 2002. Due to the increasing statistics of people having HIV is what led to the creation of the institution. As young as starting from the kids of ages 8 being exposed to such illness and having countless numbers of them in our community is what makes it difficult for us to immediately respond and solve the issue. Speaking of being exposed at an early age, it is also due to a lack of knowledge about the consequences of their actions and not having much care as to what they have done. Considering all these factors, the institution was being held to protect more citizens and live their life properly and in a secure community.

Organizer
The organization that helped found the institution is The Global Fund. Given the Resource prepared by Nathaniel Limbo, it was stated that the Global Fund has been supporting the MAAAI for several years and eventually became the organization's investor. The program itself aspires to help millions of people that suffer from common medical issues, issues such as HIV, tuberculosis, and Malaria. Years after the program reached its goal and assisted millions, more than a hundred.

Beneficiaries
The organization has been of great help and support to many individuals that have experienced medical conditions. According to the resource provided by Sir Nathaniel Limbo, clients of the organization include 2 6-12-year-olds, 100 individuals per month that range from 13-19, and over 800 people ages 20 and above. The beneficiaries that the MAAAI has helped nourish eclipses over a thousand. Truly enough, the program has indeed saved lives and helped prevent potential illnesses in Mindanao.

Benefactors
The MAAAI couldn’t have done all these wonderful deeds without the support of benefactors. The Global Fund is an essential contributor to the great cause of this program. Support from government subsidies such as the Department of Health and RHWC had played an important role in the success of the organization. Most of all, we are immensely thankful for the citizens who have donated and volunteered in the MAAAI’s programs to help the community.
